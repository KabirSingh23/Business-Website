About Us

Located in the heart of Lodhi Colony, Bloom & Bean is more than just a café. It's a sanctuary for plant lovers and coffee seekers. Founded by Rhea Kapoor in 2022, the space blends aromatic brews with curated greenery — designed to help you breathe, connect, and create.
