About Us

Located in the heart of Lodhi Colony, Bloom & Bean is a boutique café and plant shop founded by Rhea Kapoor in 2022. More than just a place for coffee, it's a living sanctuary — offering a serene environment where guests can relax, work, or reconnect with nature over a cup of beautifully brewed coffee.

Every corner is designed with love — combining rustic elements with lush greenery to create a unique biophilic experience. Whether you're a freelancer needing a creative space or a plant lover browsing our curated collection, Bloom & Bean welcomes you with warm aromas and even warmer hearts.
